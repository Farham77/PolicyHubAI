# HR Policy Document

## 1. Attendance & Time Tracking Policy

### 1.1 Working Hours
- Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (Monday to Friday).
- Employees are expected to be present during core hours (10:00 AM to 4:00 PM).

### 1.2 Late Arrival Policy
- Arrival after 10:10 AM is marked as late.
- 3 late arrivals in a single week = 0.5 day leave deduction.
- 6 late arrivals in a month = formal warning issued.

### 1.3 Absence Policy
- Absence without prior approval is marked as unpaid leave.
- If no communication is made within 24 hours, it is considered a policy violation.

---

## 2. Leave Policy

### 2.1 Casual Leave (CL)
- 12 casual leaves per year.
- Maximum 2 consecutive CLs allowed without justification.
- CL cannot be used during probation period (first 3 months).

### 2.2 Sick Leave (SL)
- 10 sick leaves per year.
- Medical certificate required if absence exceeds 2 consecutive days.
- Chronic illness cases may be reviewed separately by HR.

### 2.3 Emergency Leave
- Granted only under urgent personal circumstances.
- Requires manager + HR approval if more than 1 day.

### 2.4 Leave Combination Rules
- Sick + Casual leave combination is allowed only with HR approval.
- Leave cannot be backdated without valid justification.

---

## 3. Work From Home (WFH) Policy

- Maximum 2 WFH days per week.
- WFH must be requested at least 24 hours in advance.
- Manager approval is mandatory.
- WFH can be revoked during project deadlines or critical releases.
- Misuse (non-availability during WFH hours) leads to suspension of WFH privilege for 30 days.

---

## 4. Performance & Probation Policy

### 4.1 Probation Period
- All new employees undergo a 3-month probation period.
- Confirmation depends on performance review + manager feedback.

### 4.2 Poor Performance Handling
- First warning: verbal feedback.
- Second warning: written improvement plan (PIP).
- Failure to improve within 30 days may result in termination.

---

## 5. Code of Conduct

- Professional behavior is mandatory at all times.
- Harassment, discrimination, or toxic behavior leads to immediate disciplinary review.
- Confidential company discussions must not be shared externally.

---

## 6. Conflict Resolution
- Employees must first approach their direct manager.
- If unresolved, HR escalation is required.
- Anonymous complaints are reviewed but require supporting details.
