# Admin & Office Operations Policy

## 1. Office Attendance Rules

- Employees must check in using biometric system.
- Failure to check-in 3 times in a week triggers HR notification.

---

## 2. Office Timing Flexibility

- Flexible timing allowed between 8:30 AM – 10:30 AM.
- Core collaboration hours: 10:30 AM – 3:30 PM (mandatory presence).

---

## 3. Visitor Management Policy

- All visitors must be registered at reception.
- Visitors must wear a visitor badge at all times.
- Employees are responsible for their visitors.

---

## 4. Office Resource Usage

- Meeting rooms must be booked in advance.
- Leaving workspace unclean repeatedly may result in warning.
- Equipment damage due to negligence is chargeable.

---

## 5. Holiday & Leave Calendar

- Official holidays are published annually.
- Unplanned holidays require management approval.
- Department heads may enforce blackout periods during deadlines.

---

## 6. Workplace Behavior

- Noise levels must be controlled in open workspace.
- Mobile calls should be taken in designated areas.

---

## 7. Security Rules

- No tailgating (following someone into secure areas).
- ID card must be visible at all times inside office premises.
