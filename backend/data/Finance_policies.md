# Finance & Expense Policy

## 1. General Expense Rules

- All expenses must be pre-approved unless marked urgent.
- Expenses must be directly related to company operations.
- Personal expenses are strictly non-reimbursable.

---

## 2. Travel Policy

### 2.1 Flight Rules
- Economy class for all employees.
- Business class allowed only for senior management or 6+ hour flights.

### 2.2 Local Travel
- Taxi/ride-hailing is reimbursable for official visits only.
- Daily commute is not reimbursable.

---

## 3. Hotel Policy

- Hotel cost limits depend on employee grade:
  - Junior: budget hotels only
  - Mid-level: standard business hotels
  - Senior: premium hotels allowed within limit
- Luxury suites require CFO approval.

---

## 4. Meal & Per Diem Policy

- Daily meal allowance is capped per region.
- Alcohol is strictly non-reimbursable.
- Team meals require manager approval.

---

## 5. Reimbursement Process

- Claims must be submitted within 7 working days.
- Valid receipts are mandatory.
- Missing receipts may result in partial or rejected claims.

---

## 6. Approval Hierarchy

- <$50 → Manager approval
- $50–$500 → Department head approval
- >$500 → Finance + senior management approval

---

## 7. Fraud & Misuse Policy

- Fake receipts = immediate disciplinary action.
- Repeated misuse may lead to termination and legal action.
